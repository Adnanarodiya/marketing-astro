# Privacy Policy

## 1. General Information

Haus der Finanzen GmbH takes the protection of your personal data very seriously. We treat your personal data confidentially and in accordance with the Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (FADP / nFADP) and this Privacy Policy.

The use of our website is generally possible without providing personal data. Where personal data is collected on our website, this is always done on a voluntary basis.

## 3. Data Collected

We collect and process the following personal data:

* First and last name
* Company name (optional)
* Email address
* Telephone number (optional)
* Message content submitted through the contact form
* Technical data (IP address, browser type, access time)

## 4. Purpose of Data Processing

The collected data is used exclusively to process your inquiry, contact you, and provide our services. Your data will not be shared with third parties unless required to fulfill legal obligations.

## 5. Contact Form

If you submit inquiries via our contact form, the information you provide, including your contact details, will be stored for the purpose of processing your request and handling any follow-up questions.

We will not share this data without your consent.

## 6. Cookies

Our website may use cookies in certain areas. Cookies do not cause any damage to your computer and do not contain viruses. They help make our website more user-friendly and efficient.

Some cookies are automatically deleted when you close your browser (so-called session cookies).

You can configure your browser to notify you when cookies are set and allow cookies only in individual cases.

## 7. Data Security

We implement technical and organizational security measures to protect your data against manipulation, loss, destruction, or unauthorized access.

Our security measures are continuously improved in line with technological developments.

## 8. Your Rights Under the Swiss nFADP

Under the Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (nFADP), you have the right to:

* Obtain information about the personal data we store about you
* Request correction of inaccurate data
* Request deletion of your stored data
* Request the transfer of your data (data portability)
* Object to the processing of your data

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Swiss Federal Data Protection and Information Commissioner (FDPIC).

For any questions regarding your personal data, please contact us at: **[info@hdf.ch](mailto:info@hdf.ch)**

## 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time to ensure compliance with current legal requirements or to reflect changes to our services.

The updated Privacy Policy will apply upon your next visit to our website.
